# World-Class Homepage Research Data

## Conversion & Behavioral Science

### Eye Tracking & Attention
- **57%** of viewing time is spent above the fold (Nielsen Norman Group)
- Users form aesthetic judgments in **50 milliseconds**
- **F-pattern** scanning: left-to-right from vertical axis — ideal for product grids
- **Z-pattern** scanning: diagonal across blocks — ideal for hero + CTA layouts
- Viewing time above fold dropped from 80% (2010) to 57% (2018) — but still dominates

### Conversion Benchmarks
- Average eCommerce conversion rate: **3.65%**
- Luxury eCommerce conversion rate: **0.94%**
- Average bounce rate: **38-47%** overall; **51%** on mobile
- Mobile conversion: **2.9%** vs desktop **4.8%**
- Mobile accounts for **70%+** of traffic

### Value Proposition
- Homepage must answer "What's in it for me?" in **under 5 seconds**
- Benefit-focused messaging outperforms feature lists
- One store improved conversion from **2.1% to 3.8%** by repositioning visual hierarchy alone

## Social Proof & Trust

### Reviews & Ratings
- **93%** of consumers say online reviews impact purchase decisions
- **270%** conversion increase when products display 5+ reviews
- **67%** more purchases when customer reviews are visible
- **80%** conversion increase from video testimonials vs text
- **12.5%** conversion rate on pages with testimonials vs 11.4% without
- **86%** of buying decisions influenced by visible star ratings
- Optimal rating range: **4.0-4.7 stars** (perfect 5.0 hurts trust)

### Trust Badges
- Trust signals at checkout lift conversions by up to **32%**
- Security badges near form fields outperform header/footer placement by **2-3x**
- McAfee Secure badge increased conversions by **1%**
- 30-day money-back guarantee boosted sales by **32.57%**
- Unfamiliar badges **decreased trust by 12%** — recognition matters
- **19%** of cart abandonment from credit card security concerns
- **81%** of shoppers feel concerned on unfamiliar sites
- Store rating widgets missing from **76%** of stores (biggest easy win)

### Testimonial Placement
- Most effective when placed **near CTAs**
- Video testimonials outperform text by **80%**
- Named + photo attribution increases trust significantly

## Newsletter Signup Placement
- Moving signup to prominent header position increased conversions by **27.8%**
- Below-the-fold works for complex/high-commitment offers
- Bottom-of-page captures **highly engaged visitors** who scrolled
- Short forms (name + email only) convert highest

## Visual Rhythm & Spacing
- Establish rhythm, then **deliberately break it** to highlight key elements
- Generous negative space around CTAs makes them pop
- Alternate full-width and contained sections for engagement
- Consistent spacing between sections; vary visual treatment for rhythm
- Each section's layout should guide toward conversion points

## Luxury-Specific Insights

### Top Brand Patterns (Rolex, Patek Philippe, Omega, AP)
- **Heritage/story** is central — not optional
- Product showcased through **immersive photography**, not e-commerce grids
- **Brand-first layouts** — aesthetic over convention
- **Museum, manufacture, artisan sections** build aspiration
- **Service commitment** (lifetime warranty) is a selling point
- **Retail locator** drives offline conversion

### Luxury Homepage Must-Haves
1. Cinematic hero (video preferred)
2. Featured collections (not just "all products")
3. Best sellers / iconic models
4. Heritage / brand story block
5. Craftsmanship storytelling
6. Lifestyle banner
7. Trust / credentials (authenticity, materials, shipping)
8. Editorial / journal (SEO + authority)
9. Testimonials / reviews
10. Newsletter with "exclusive" positioning
11. Footer with store locator, service, legal

## The AIDA Model Applied to Homepages

| Stage | Goal | Sections | Key metric |
|-------|------|----------|------------|
| **Attention** | Stop the scroll | Hero, Credentials bar | Bounce rate |
| **Interest** | Explore further | Categories, Products | Scroll depth, CTR |
| **Desire** | Want it | Banner, Story, Trust | Time on page, add-to-cart |
| **Action** | Convert | Journal, Testimonials, Newsletter | Conversion, signup rate |
